Micheal Jackson Prince Usher

Usher Raymond IV

Born: October 14, 1978
Record label: Originally LaFace Records

Hit songs:

  1. Love In This Club
  2. OMG
  3. Yeah!

Biography

Usher was born in Texas to non-musician parents, but mostly lived in Tennesse. He joined the church choir due to his grandma, then joined a singing group. His family realized he had a talent for signing, and decided to encourage him to pursue music, and did so by moving to Atlanta, Georgia, where beginner singers could start their careers.

Notable Contributions:

  • He was Billboard's 2nd most successful artist of the 2000s
  • Has sold over 100 million records
  • He was the first artist since The Beatles to have the top 2 spots on the Year-End Hot 100 chart

Famous Quote:

"Success is about dedication. You may not be where you want to be or do what you want to do when you're on the journey. But you've got to be willing to have vision and foresight that leads you to an incredible end."
