Biography

Price was born in Minneapolis to jazz singer and pianist parents, making him exposed to music at an early age. He was always encouraged with his interest in music, and wrote his first song as 7 years old. His parents unfortunately divorced, and he lived with his dad due to issues with his step-sibling. Despite getting kicked out by his dad later on, he still attended high school and focused on improving his dancing skills and instrument-playing.

Notable Contributions:

  • Sold over 150 million records worldwide
  • Was given a Doctor of Humane Letters by the University of Minnesota
  • Inducted into the Black Music & Entertainment Walk of Fame in 2022
