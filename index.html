Micheal Jackson

Born: August 29, 1958
Record label: Originally Motown Records

Hit songs:

  1. Billie Jean
  2. Beat it
  3. Rock With You

Biography

Micheal Jackson was born in Indiana to a large family; he had 9 other siblings. He would grow up to play in the Jackson 5, a band composed of him and his siblings, debuting with Motown Records. He would later debut on his solo career, his sixth album "Thriller" becoming the best-selling album in the world. His contributions to music are still moticeable even today.

Notable Contributions:

  • Is considered to be the "King of Pop" due to his immense influence
  • Was given a Presidential Public Safety Commendation by Ronald Reagen due to his humanitarian work
  • Helped contribute to racial diversity

Famous Quote:

"In a world filled with hate, we must still dare to hope"
